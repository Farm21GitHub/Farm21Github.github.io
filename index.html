Our Mission

Farm21 is on a mission to revolutionize agriculture through smart technology, making farming efficient, sustainable, and accessible to everyone—from urban dwellers to large-scale agribusinesses. With IoT, AI, and data-driven automation, we provide affordable, plug-and-play solutions that optimize resources, increase yields, and empower farmers with actionable insights.

Why It Matters

  • 50% of India’s workforce depends on agriculture, yet productivity remains low due to inefficient water use, climate change, and lack of technology.
  • Water scarcity affects 54% of Indian farmland, making precision irrigation critical for sustainable farming.
  • The smart agriculture market is projected to grow from $76.6M in 2023 to $218.9M by 2030 (CAGR 16.2%), showing a massive demand for tech-driven solutions.
  • By integrating IoT sensors, automated irrigation, and AI-driven analytics, Farm21 is building the future of farming—one that is smart, efficient, and sustainable.

The Problem We Solve

Agriculture’s Growing Challenges

  • Water Wastage – Traditional irrigation methods waste up to 60% of water, depleting resources.
  • Climate Change Impact – Erratic weather patterns make crop planning unpredictable.
  • Labor Shortages – Farming remains labor-intensive, discouraging new generations from entering the sector.
  • Technology Gap – Many small and mid-sized farms lack access to precision agriculture tools.
  • Post-Harvest Losses – Nearly 30-40% of produce is lost due to poor storage and market inefficiencies.

How Farm21 Solves These Issues

  • IoT-enabled sensors provide real-time environmental data for optimized resource use.
  • AI-driven automation reduces reliance on manual labor while increasing efficiency.
  • Smart irrigation and nutrient delivery systems cut water wastage by up to 50%.
  • Data-driven insights empower farmers to predict and prevent issues before they occur.
  • Farm21 transforms farming into a science, ensuring higher yields, lower costs, and greater sustainability.

Technology & Solutions

Advanced Technology Integration

  • IoT Sensors – Monitor soil moisture, temperature, humidity, and light levels in real time.
  • AI & Machine Learning – Predict irrigation needs, detect crop health issues, and optimize nutrient supply.
  • Automated Irrigation & Fertigation – Smart water and fertilizer delivery reduces waste and maximizes yield.
  • Mobile App & Cloud Connectivity – Farmers can monitor and control farm conditions remotely.
  • Scalable Design – Solutions work for home gardens, urban farms, greenhouses, and large-scale fields.
